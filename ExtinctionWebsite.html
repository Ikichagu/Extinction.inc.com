Extinction

A game of innovation and survival

This tale starts off with a dinosaur named Robert, who's trying to escape the many asteriods and obstacles in his way.
This will require a lot of grit and you, player can help him achieve his goal.

How do I play?

The game consists of 3 main concepts

  1. Grab the meat

    2. Over the course of the game different types of meat will be thrown at Robert. The goal is to collect as much me as you can as it increases
    your overall score.

  2. Dodge the asteroids

    3. they will kill Robert!, and virtually stop you from playing the game. Dodge the many different sizes and shapes

  3. Survive as long as possible!

    4. The longer your in the game the better you will do and the more points you score. Beware though! it will
    get much harder as the game goes on.

  4. Controls

    5. Only controls you need to worry about are the left and right arrow keys. These will move the dinosaur left and right corresponding to the arrown key.
